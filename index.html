Stud of the Spuds

Become the Spudliest!

Take the role of a valiant warrior battling to become the spudliest stud the reknowned Potato Arena has ever seen! Armed with only your trusty Spud Cannon, battle an onslaught of tubers to obtain your well-deserved title.

  • Overview
    Kill enemies with your mighty Spud Cannon to earn points. Be careful, your enemies are also armed with potato guns (although they clearly aren't as cool as yours). Earn bonus points by knocking their weak-tubers clean out of the air! Once one of your potato-based munitions hits an object (yes, any object) it becomes cold. Your ammo will need to be hot upon reaching its target to apply its damage (and score those much needed points!). At the end of all five waves, you'll be rewarded a title based on the number of points you've earned. Rack up enough points to become the Stud of the Spuds and take your seat on the revered Starchy Throne!
  • Controls

    Action:

    Input:

    Description:

    Movement

    W / A / S / D

    Y'know, walking around and stuff. Shouldn't you know how that works?

    Looking Around

    Mouse Movement

    Just move the mouse around to change your point of view. It's just like moving your head around in real life.

    Shooting

    Left Mouse Button

    This fires whatever primary weapon you currently have active. Show them what your Spud Cannon can do!

    Shield

    Right Mouse Button

    Hold down the right mouse button to shield yourself from incoming damage. The shield will block your weapon, so don't even think about shooting while it's up! If your shield breaks or you stop using it, it'll recharge by itself after a short delay. Handy for those big skirmishes later on.

    Changing Primary Weapons

    Mouse Wheel

    Scroll your mouse wheel to switch between your primary weapons. Don't worry, the T.A.T.E.R will automatically adapt to whatever you're using it for. Just choose your weapon and go ham! (Bacon goes great with potatoes... Just sayin'...)

    Restarting

    Enter

    Running low on resources? Got a bad spawn? Maybe you're just a wimp?! No matter what the reason, mash that enter key to load yourself up an entirely new arena!

    Name:

    Type:

    Description:

    T.A.T.E.R

    Gun

    The Totally Awesome Tuber Expelling Rifle is a heavily modified Spud Cannon that shoots out a single potato that deals a large amount of damage to the first foe hit. Good for trying to take down one target at a time. It can also be modified to shoot out different kinds of spuds, so be on the lookout for weapon mods!

    Scatter Fries

    Gun

    A modification to the T.A.T.E.R allows it to dice its tubers on their way out. The Scatter Fries mod shoots out a random number of french fries in a spread-shot. Fries deal reduced damage individually, but have a higher chance of hitting multiple targets or hitting the same target multiple times. Good for fighting off swarms of enemies or bursting down one.
